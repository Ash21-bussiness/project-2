A little bit about Products

GOODS In economics, goods are anything that is good, usually in the sense that it provides welfare or utility to someone.Goods are the result of the secondary sector of the economy which involves the transformation of raw materials or intermediate goods into goods.

Commercial goods are construed as tangible products that are manufactured and then made available for supply to be used in an industry of commerce. Goods' diversity allows for their classification into different categories based on distinctive characteristics, such as tangibility and (ordinal) relative elasticity.
